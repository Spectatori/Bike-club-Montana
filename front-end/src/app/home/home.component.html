Welcome to the Adventure Cycling Club

Ride Together, Explore Together

Join us at the Adventure Cycling Club, where passion for cycling meets the thrill of adventure. Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or just starting out, our club offers a welcoming community for riders of all skill levels.

About Us

• Founded in 2010, the Adventure Cycling Club has grown into a vibrant community of cycling enthusiasts. Our mission is to promote a healthy lifestyle, foster camaraderie among cyclists, and explore the beauty of our surroundings on two wheels.

What We Offer

• Weekly Rides: Participate in our organized rides every weekend, ranging from leisurely trails to challenging routes.

• Cycling Events: Join our special events, including charity rides, bike races, and scenic tours.

• Community: Be part of a supportive and friendly group of cyclists who share your passion.

• Adventure: Discover new routes and trails with experienced ride leaders.
